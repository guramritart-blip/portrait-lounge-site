Newport Beach FAQ

Do you serve all of Newport Beach?

Yes. We cover all Newport Beach neighborhoods and venues including Corona del Mar, Newport Coast, Balboa Island, Lido Isle, and the Newport Center business district. Travel within Orange County is included in all packages.

Can you set up outdoors?

We can work in covered outdoor spaces with shade. For oceanfront venues, we typically set up in a shaded patio, tent, or indoor space to ensure consistent lighting quality and protect equipment from coastal conditions.

Do you work with luxury event planners?

Yes. We regularly collaborate with Newport Beach's top event planners and wedding coordinators. Our team integrates seamlessly with vendor teams at venues like Pelican Hill, Balboa Bay Resort, and private estates throughout the area.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking 2 to 6 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (April through October) and the holiday event season. Newport Beach dates fill quickly. Contact us to check availability.
