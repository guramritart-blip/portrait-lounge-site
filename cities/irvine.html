Irvine FAQ

Do you serve all of Irvine?

Yes. We cover all Irvine neighborhoods and venues including Woodbridge, Northwood, Quail Hill, University Park, and the Irvine Business Complex. Travel within Orange County is included in all packages.

How much space do you need for setup?

Our studio setup requires approximately 10x10 feet of space. We work with venue coordinators to find the ideal location that's visible to guests but doesn't disrupt the flow of your event.

Can you match our corporate branding?

Absolutely. Our Bespoke Experience includes custom backdrop options and branded elements. Many Irvine tech companies and startups use Portrait Lounge for team events and product launches with full brand integration.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking 2 to 6 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (April through October) and the holiday corporate event season. Contact us to check availability for your date.
